The story

This game is a 3D remake of our previous game : the orb.
The story starts with a shipwreck on a island.
You would need to find food, water and shelter to survive.
At night, monsters will begin to appear to appear and
you will need to fight back in order to survive.
The orb is a RPG shooter with two types of levels : day and night.
The player must defeat all the monsters to move on the next level.
